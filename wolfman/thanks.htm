Thanks for your tribute to Wolfman Jack.  I visited the legend's
grave in Belvidere this week and it was truly an emotional
experience for me.   As a jock in a couple of majors including
LA during Bob's career, he effected me as he did so many
others.   His daughter Joy was tragically killed in an auto
wreck a couple of months ago and is buried beside the man
in the side lawn of a run-down, weed infested old home where
his wife of 28-year Lu resides in what appears to me to be
abject poverty.   We are working with a family friend, Bruce
Mayo, on a film project that will do Wolf justice!  As Lu owns
the rights to "Have Mercy", this is the working title, however the
story will be greatly expanded as Lu has some really incredible
stories that never were printed and Bob has a huge library of
home films that have never seen the light of day.  If you can
imagine this.  Bruce took me to a ramshackled building that
once was Bob's working studio.  Inside,...filing cabinets of
hand-written liners that he used on his shows.  What an
experience!  Wolfman Jack is gone but not forgotten and
be on the lookout for a feature film that will tear the entertainment
industry a new one.   Thanks for your tribute Stinky, have
mercy and stay tuned!
 
Johnny West
CIC Film and Video Productions
